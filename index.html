Hydraulic Engineering

Ten months of floods have taught your engineers to fight the river with walls of earth and brick. You can now build Levees.
🔒CLOSE the gate: blocks small floods. Crops are safe — but no fresh silt reaches your fields.
🔓OPEN the gate: water flows in, drowning crops — but it leaves rich black silt behind.
⚠️A massive flood can smash a closed gate — but a freshly rebuilt levee is packed hard and will hold for a good while.
